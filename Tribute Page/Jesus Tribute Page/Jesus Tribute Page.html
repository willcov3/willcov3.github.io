Steps for Salvation:

1. Believe with your heart that Jesus Christ is Lord

There is One God, the Creator, who sent His Son, Jesus, to save the world from our sins by paying the ultimate sacrifice. He was born of the Virgin Mary, conceived by the Holy Spirit, which makes Him fully human and fully God.

2. Confess with your mouth

Now that you believe, acknowledge with your mouth that Jesus is Lord. If you are still struggling with belief, pray to the Lord by entering a meditative state and speak from your heart or say, "Lord, help me to believe in you. Show me your way. Allow Jesus Christ to change my heart, my will, and desires, so I can show the world love. I have a small mustard seed of faith; make it grow Lord! In Jesus name I pray, Amen.

3. Repent your sins and turn away from evil

No one is perfect or will ever be perfect. God wants us to turn away from sins because they bring destruction to our lives. The commands in the Bible are guidelines, that are almost unrealistic to follow, but we are to sacrifice our burning desires to bring love to this dark and hopeless world.
